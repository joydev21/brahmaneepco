2023


Braided River Aid - Hydro Morphological Analyzer 1D Dam Release Flow Forecasting Model (BRAHMA 1D DRFF)


BRAHMA 1D DRFF USER MANUAL

River modeling is a complex and least understood process or phenomenon in nature. It is difficult to find analytical solutions for most problems in river engineering, and it is very tedious to obtain numerical solutions without the help of high-speed computers. Therefore, prior to the 1970s, many river engineering problems had to be solved through field investigations and laboratory physical models (also called scale models). With the recent advancements in computer technology, computational models have been greatly improved and widely applied to solve real-life problems. Computational river dynamics is now considered a branch of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). It solves river engineering problems using numerical methods.
